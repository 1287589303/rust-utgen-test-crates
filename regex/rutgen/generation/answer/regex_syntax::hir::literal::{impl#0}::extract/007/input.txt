*hir.kind() must be of type Literal(hir::Literal(ref bytes)), Class(hir::Class::Bytes(ref cls)), or Class(hir::Class::Unicode(ref cls)); bytes must be a non-empty Vec<u8>, cls must contain valid byte or unicode character ranges, and ensures min/max repetition constraints (0 to 10 for min, None or up to 10 for max).
